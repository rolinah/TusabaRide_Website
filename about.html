Our Story

About Tusaba Ride

We believe every university student in Uganda deserves safe, affordable transport — at a tap or a USSD code, day or night. Tusaba Ride is a student-focused venture built through university partnerships, on a mission to become the most trusted campus transport platform in Uganda and beyond.

Our Mission

To redefine how students move around university environments by creating the most trusted, inclusive platform — with both app and USSD access for all students.

🌍

Our Vision

Driving campus mobility forward by transforming student transport to be safe, affordable, and reliable — across every university in Uganda.

Our People

Our Riders Community

Our riders are vetted through university institutional processes — not just background checks. When joining Tusaba Ride, they commit to verified pickup points, ride logging, and accountability, giving students and their families genuine peace of mind.
